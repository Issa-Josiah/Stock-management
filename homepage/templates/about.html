IMS

An Inventory Management System

| About |

IMS is a simple inventory management software created for the needs of a company. It keeps track of incoming and outgoing stock with dynamically generated bills. Each supplier gets their own profile to view their transaction history.

| Features |

  • Interactive stock chart
  • Track current available stock
  • Record incoming transactions via purchases
  • Record outgoing transactions via sales
  • Save all transaction history
  • Auto-generated printable bill
  • Dedicated supplier profiles
